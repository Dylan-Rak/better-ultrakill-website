Weapons

ULTRAKILL has many diffrent weapons; there are your 5 standard
weapons, 3 varients per weapon, 3 alterante weapons and 4 arms
each varient changes the alternate fire of the weapon and the
alternate weapons change the primary fire.

Revolver


The revolver is the first weapon you get in the game, it has three varients and an alterante.

The blue varient is called the piercer, you can hold alt fire to charge a high damage hit scan.

The green revolver is called the marksman, with alt fire you can toss a coin that, when shot with a
hit scan will ricochet into the nearest enemy and increase its damage.

The red revolver is called the sharpshooter, holding alt fire you'll spin the revolver around your
finger and on release o alt fire a hit scan will be shot out and ricochet of walls. the amount of ricochets
is dependent on how long you spin the revolver.

the alterante revolver has been dubbed the slab revolver by the community due to how you obtain it
in every level of the first layer of the game, limbo, you can find buttons that when all are pressed a door
will open revealing the revolver. the slab revolver simply fires slower, but does more damage.

Shotgun

The shotgun is the second weapon in the game, much like the recolver it has 3 varients and an alterante.

The blue shotgun is called the core eject, holding alt fire will allow you to shoot out a grenade that explodes on impact.

The green shotgun is the pump charge shotgun, alt fire will pump the shot gun increasing the number of pellets shot out,
but pump it too much and it will instead explode when fired.

The red shotgun is the sawed-on, it adds a chainsaw to the front of the gun.

The alternate shotgun is the Jackhammer, the primary fire is changed to a melee attack that increases in damage the faster your moving

The alterante blue shotgun is slightly changed where instead of shooting the grenade out it is ejected right in front of you.
