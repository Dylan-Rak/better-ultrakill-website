About

ULTRAKILL is an indie, movement, first person shooter inspired by games like DMC and Quake.
The game is made by Arsi "Hakita" Patala, and published by New Blood, the game is currently in
early access with the 8th layer (chapter) fraud is set to come out sometime this year.
Rip apart your foes with varied destructive weapons and shower in their blood to regain your health.
For more information go to: ULTRAKILL Wiki

New Blood

New Blood Website
New Blood Ineractive, founded in 2014, orginaly began development of virtual reality games.
However, these plans never materialized. At the time, New Blood was looking for a pixel artist
and Clinkscales needed a publisher for his Kickstarter-backed game Super Galaxy Squadron,
so they agreed that New Blood would publish the game and Clinkscales join the company in return.
this was the start of New Blood as a publisher and they would later go on to publish many more games.
notable titles include: Dusk, ULTRAKILL, Faith and Amid Evil
